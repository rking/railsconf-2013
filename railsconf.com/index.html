RailsConf 2013

RailsConf, the largest gathering of Ruby and Rails developers in the world, is returning to Portland, OR! Join us from April 29th - May 2nd and connect with top Rails talent, companies, and project owners from around the world.

Welcome, everyone!

It's here! We're trying to ensure that RailsConf is a welcoming, inclusive place for everyone, so to that end please take a look at our anti-harassment policy. We expect all attendees, sponsors, volunteers, staff, and speakers to respect the other participants at the conference and abide by the policy.
